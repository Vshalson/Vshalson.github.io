COMPLIANCE (In Brief)

GRC : Governance Risk and Compliance

SOX : Sarbanes Oxley Act [2002], it established rule to protect public from fraud practices of corporations and business entities. It require all financial reports to include an internal controls report

SOC : Service Organization Control provides detailed information and assurance about service organization security and privacy control. It conducted by external service org

Types of SOC audits and report

Type 1 : audit and report carried out on specified date

Type 2 : audit and report carried out over specified period, min 6 months

SOC2 audit include :

Description of system or services

Test of controls and results of testing

Opinion letter

PCI-DSS : Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (2004), if you process credit card or transit credit card data it applies to you.

ISO : International Organization for Standardization, applies to business irrespective of size or industry they cater to. To obtain it, org have to prove requirements.

NIST : National Instiute of Standards and Technology , is policy framework which includes best practices for keeping data secure.

GLBA : Gramm Leach Billey Act, US federal law that requires financial institutions to explain how they share and protect their customers' private information.

CCPA : California Consumer Privacy Act, regulates how all over the world allowed to handle Personal information of California residents.

NYDFS : NY Department of Financial Services, set of regulations from the New York Department of Financial Services that places new cybersecurity requirements on financial institutions.

Takeaway

One can do risk analysis of Organisations, Technical Solutions, on people or anything.

It include thing to eliminate the risk or sometimes to reduce the risk exposure of vulnerability present if exploit

Standard workflow

  • One have to do audit iteration annually or bi-annually.

Suppose, org want to use Salesforce.

What is the risk to org to start to use that system. It's cloud based platform.

  1. How we gonna use that?
    1. What is there tech stack like?
    2. What is the use of it?
    3. do they have SOC type 2 Compliance?
    4. Can you they recent pentest report?
  2. Now taking pivot, what if org really wants to use salesforce?
    1. How they gonna use that? And expand intel in it
    2. whose using it? Is it marketing dept, sales dept. , CEO or everyone in org, or third party vendors
    3. what is data protection? Information security not cyber security
  3. Two Risk Assessment Technique
    1. Quantitative
    2. Qualitative
      1. More Subjective
      2. FAIR : Risk Assessment Model
  4. Document in plan of Actions and Milestones

    What Risks are? what there remediations be? who's gonna be responsible making remediations? when those remediations gonna be made and strict follow up on it.
